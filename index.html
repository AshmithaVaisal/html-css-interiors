About Espace Studio

Welcome to Espace Studio, where innovation meets elegance in interior design. Our mission is to transform your spaces into stunning, functional environments that reflect your unique style and vision. With a passion for design and a commitment to excellence, we create interiors that inspire and delight.

Our Story

Espace Studio was founded in 2023 with a clear vision: to bring a fresh perspective to interior design. Over the years, we have grown from a small, passionate team into a renowned design studio known for our creativity and attention to detail. Our portfolio includes a diverse range of projects, from cozy residential homes to sophisticated commercial spaces.

Our Philosophy

At Espace Studio, we believe that great design enhances the way you live and work. Our approach is holistic, blending aesthetics with functionality to create spaces that are both beautiful and practical. We prioritize sustainability, incorporating eco-friendly materials and practices to ensure our designs are as kind to the planet as they are stunning to look at.

Our Team

Our talented team of designers, architects, and artisans is led by Ar.Ashmitha Vaisal R, whose vision and expertise guide every project. Each member of Espace Studio brings a unique set of skills and a shared passion for design. Our collaborative approach ensures that every project benefits from a diverse range of ideas and perspectives, resulting in truly unique and personalized spaces.
